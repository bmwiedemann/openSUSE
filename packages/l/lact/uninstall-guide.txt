LACT Uninstallation Guide
=========================

After uninstalling LACT you can manually remove your user from the "lact" group if desired:

- sudo gpasswd -d $USER lact

Log out, wait 10-15 seconds before logging back in.

If you enabled AMD Overclocking, you can saftely remove
the kernel boot parameter in your bootloader configuration,
regenerate it and reboot.
